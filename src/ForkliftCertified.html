Forklift Certified

In a world driven by AI one trucker named Dave refuses to be ruled by his robot overlords. Unfortunately for this causes Dave to lose his cushy trucking job since AI is said to be more “Efficent” and “Better” at doing his job. Naturally Dave moved on and started working at a warehouse as a forklift operator. The robot menace followed Dave to his new occupation and is once again threatening to take his job. One thing the AI’s didnt count on was Dave’s willingness to Fight Back!

  • Players shoot at advancing robot enemies trying to survive as long as possible
  • Players advance through the game by picking up items and killing enough enemies to fight the final boss
  • WASD to move Trucker Dave, Arrow Keys to shoot in said direction
